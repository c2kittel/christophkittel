We are thrilled to announce the official start of the **MOAT** (*Multi-disciplinary Observations of Antarctic ice-shelf Thresholds*) project! 

Funded by **BELSPO**, this ambitious 4-year project brings together experts from **ULB, KU Leuven, and VUB** to better understand the stability of Antarctic ice shelves. 

As **Co-PI** at the VUB, I am currently overseeing the preparation of the advanced ground-based sensors that will be deployed on the **Roi Baudouin Ice Shelf**. Our goal is to measure basal and surface melting with unprecedented precision to improve climate models.

Stay tuned for more updates as we prepare for the first field season!
